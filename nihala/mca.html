MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS (2 YEAR COURSE)

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a 2-year (Four Semester) professional master’s degree in Computer Applications. The course is designed to meet the growing demand for qualified professionals in the field of IT. The curriculum is designed for developing application software to various domains and requirements by accommodating the latest trends in the industry.

Started in the year 1999, with an intake of 30 students, this is the first MCA program under the self-financing sector in Kerala, initially under the affiliation of University of Calicut and now under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. MCA at MESCE is currently having an intake of 60 students. Our program under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, aims at imparting comprehensive knowledge with equal emphasis on theory and practical. State-of-the-art Lab facilities and infrastructure, eminent faculty and education -conducive environment favours our students to concentrate in their studies and develop into competitive and compassionate professionals.

VISION

To facilitate transformation of students into competent professionals with human values and social commitment.

MISSION

To equip students to face the industry challenges. To inculcate leadership qualities, human values and social responsibilities in the youth to make them leaders in nation building. To build research interest in students and thereby contribute meaningfully to the prosperity of the nation.
