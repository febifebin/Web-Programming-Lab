MES ENGINEERING COLLEGE KUTTIPURAM

M.E.S College of Engineening, the first Engineering College established under the self financing sector in Kerala is situated on the beautiful and serene banks of Nila or Bharathapuzha. It was established as an institution with minority status in 1994. At present there areeight undergraduate programmes including B Arch and ten post graduate courses in M Tech, MCA, MBA and M Arch In the college.Nearly 3000 students study here under the guidance of over 240 qualified faculty members and research guides.
